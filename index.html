Description:



Simple, healthy Indian recipes and videos based on natural living on our farmhouse and around its surroundings of dense forest, Odisha, India. Our names are Ms. Aneez Fatima ( Nickname - Anee ) and Ms. Aneeza Fatima ( Nickname - Anna ) We are the host of this channel. We focus on our hobbies that is creating and posting recipe videos and are also very much interested in Organic and natural farming for which we were inspired by our grandparents ( Mr. Godfrey and Ms. Bernadine Francis ). We are also pursuing our higher studies. We live in Mumbai that is the most busiest commercial city of India and that is why we sometimes like to explore the countryside life for lush greenery and peaceful inner-feeling on our beautiful farmhouse in the dense forest of Odisha, India. Love You all. Stay connected with us.
